About Us

We are based out of New Delhi, India, Seven Safar is a tour and travel agency. We specialize in offering a range of wildlife and vacation packages that will match with the taste of travellers. We believe that it is not just reaching the destination, rather it is the journey that makes the soul relive again. We are here to guide you with the beauty, know about the rich culture and experience the charm of the place you visit. As a leading tour orator in the heart of India, we are dedicated to make you go through every corner of the country through our various types of packages, from sun kissed mountains of Himalayas to sun basking in the beach of Goa.

Our name “Seven Safar” signifies our promise to craft your Safar or Journey which are diverse as the number 7 signifies. Having worked with thousands of clients worldwide for more than ten years and building long-lasting relationships, we have established and accomplished numerous milestones in India. This shows how our team has a deep knowledge and the passion they have as travel executives who ensure the journey to be smooth, thoughtful and feels like home.

We have meticulously made the finest travel routes, accommodations, and experiences so you can explore every nook and cranny of India’s beauty without worrying about budget, convenience, or logistics. As every traveller has their own choice of interest who might seek in watching the Royal Bengal and experience the luxury and grandeur or packages for the intrepid explore for the wild adventurous part, we offer everything.

Our Mission

is simple yet overwhelming where we provide you the path to the most modern, high quality and affordable travel solution in India. We have a very fundamental principle, that is to offer reasonable, efficient and excellent travel experience in India. Our ultimate goal is to offer our global clients easy access to India’s panoramic landscapes, vibrant cultures, and breathtaking natural wonders.

Our Excellence

Lies in our approach, as we are not only planners rather innovators in the industry of travel and tourism. You can find a streamlined platform that we have created where your dream destination is just a click away from you. But beyond technology the main goal our team focuses is on the traveller. We ensure round the clock customer services ensuring very less error so that your only focus will be on the memories you are creating.
