Tadoba Online Jeep Safari Booking

Embarking on a Jeep Safari in Tadoba National Park offers an unparalleled experience for those with a passion for exploration. The journey through the dense reserve is exceptionally rewarding, with the likelihood of spotting tigers and other wild animals being higher compared to other regions. The open jeep safari accommodates up to six people, along with a guide and driver.

For booking a jeep safari in Tadoba, we recommend reserving online in advance. If you are unable to book online, you may contact us directly for assistance.

Best Time to Visit – March to May is optimal for sightings. Monsoon (June–September) is partially closed. October–November has vibrant greenery. December–February is pleasant.

Important Information about Jeep Safari in Tadoba
  • Choose the gate for safari as per your accommodation.
  • Book your permit through the gates close to your resort.
  • All buffer gates will be closed on Wednesday from Feb 1.
  • Visitors must carry entry permits and valid ID proof.
  • Permits are provisional and subject to change/cancellation.
  • Follow Tadoba Tiger Reserve guidelines as directed.
  • Registered guides are pre-decided; changes are not permitted.
Online Jeep Safari Booking Procedure
  • Provide full name, age, sex of each visitor as per ID.
  • Select safari timing (Morning/Evening).
  • Provide a valid ID number (Voter ID, Aadhaar, Driving License, etc.).
  • Safari entry fee is paid in advance.
  • Jeep & Canter safari amounts are non-refundable.
  • All visitors must follow park rules & regulations.
