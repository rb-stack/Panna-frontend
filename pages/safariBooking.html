Panna Online Jeep Safari Booking

Pack your bags and get ready for the Safari of your life. A jeep safari through Panna National Park is your gateway to exploring a spectacular landscape revived as a beacon of tiger conservation. Your journey into this dense, beautiful forest promises thrilling wildlife sightings, from the majestic tiger and elusive leopard to sloth bears, wild dogs, and a rich variety of birds.

The Panna safari booking process is managed by the Madhya Pradesh Forest Department across five distinct zones: Madla, Hinouta, Akola Core, Akola, and Jinna. Each zone offers a unique perspective of the park's diverse ecosystem. To ensure a fair and quality experience for all, expert guides and vehicles are allocated through a computerized lottery system on the day of your safari.

We highly recommend you book Panna safari rides early through our online booking platform. With only 20 jeeps permitted per zone per shift, advanced reservation is essential, especially during the peak season from October to June. Our seamless online process ensures you avoid disappointment and guarantees your place in this incredible wilderness.

Best Time to Visit Panna

The best time to visit Panna National Park is from October 15th to March 15th. As it is wintertime, the weather is pleasant and cool, making it suitable for the ride. You get to spot animals like tigers, deer, sambar, leopard, and many birds while you enjoy the safari If you visit in the summer months, that is from April to June, it gets very hot, but you might see more animals near water sources. However, the heat can make long safaris uncomfortable. Given the tropical climate of Tadoba National Park, the period from particularly in May, is considered the optimal time for tiger sightings, as the summer heat is at its peak. The monsoon season spans from June to September when the national park is partially closed. is also a favorable time to visit, as the park's greenery is at its most vibrant. is another popular period, as the weather remains pleasant. You can choose the timing of your visit based on your preferred wildlife experience. The park is closed during the monsoon season, from July to September, due to heavy rains. This makes the movement of vehicles difficult, and the rain helps the forest to regenerate and become greener and beautiful.

Online Jeep Safari Booking Procedure for Panna National Park

To book your jeep safari in Panna National Park, please follow these steps:

  • Bring official govt. ID proof and give full name, age, and gender.
  • Mention the timing of the safari that you want to choose- afternoon or morning.
  • Aadhar Card, Voter ID, Driving License, or Passport for foreign nationals is required.
  • Pay the full amount online in advance to get the booking confirmation.
  • All the fees are non-refundable and non-transferable once booked.
  • All visitors must carry the original ID used for booking during the safari and are required to follow the park's rules and regulations.

Panna Tiger Safari Booking: Key Points to Remember

  • All payments are required to be made in advance and are refundable and non-transferable once paid.
  • A valid government ID (Aadhar, Voter ID, etc.) is mandatory for all visitors. Foreign nationals must provide passport details.
  • The park is closed every Wednesday and from July 1st to September 30th for the monsoon.
  • You must carry the original ID used for booking and arrive at the gate at least 10 minutes before your scheduled safari time.
  • Safaris can be booked up to 120 days in advance. Early booking is highly recommended.
