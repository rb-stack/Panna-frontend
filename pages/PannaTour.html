Tour Overview

Package 01 - Explore Panna: 2 Nights 3 Days

If you love animals and wildlife, this place is just for you. This special tour of 2 nights and 3 days includes a wide range of safari activities. We will cover all the zones these days both core zone and buffer zones. We will make you come through the gate based on the recent animal spotting and sightings report. This way there will be a maximum chance for you to spot the wildlife , birds and have a great jungle experience. With our expert guides and experienced drivers your journey will be safe, fun and memorable.

Highlights of the tour

  • One of the tiger reserves and UNESCO heritage sites.
  • Planned safaris in different zones based on daily sighting updates
  • Know the insights with drivers and guides.
  • Stay in between the forest and listening to the birds every morning

Tour Itinerary

Day 1
Arrive in Panna

You will arrive at Panna National Park and check into your wildlife-themed resort. The resort overlooks the beautiful forest. After a delicious lunch you can explore the nearby town of Panna. You may visit attractions like the historic Panna Fort or the beautiful Pandav Falls. In the evening, you can relax and enjoy the facilities at the resort. Dinner and overnight stay will be at the resort.

Day 2
Morning and Afternoon Safari

Early in the morning, you will board a safari gypsy for an exciting game drive. A field breakfast will be provided during the safari. After the morning safari, you will return to the resort to relax. After lunch, you will head out again for another thrilling jungle safari. The evening is free for you to enjoy the resortâ€™s facilities. You will have your dinner in the resort and then stay for the night.

Day 3
Depart for Onward Journey

After breakfast, you will check out from the resort and begin your journey back home. You will carry with you sweet and wild memories of your amazing tour at Panna National Park.
